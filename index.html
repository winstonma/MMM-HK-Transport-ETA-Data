Field Descriptions:

  • stop: Unique stop identifier
  • name_tc/en/sc: Stop names in Traditional Chinese, English, Simplified Chinese
  • lat/long: GPS coordinates
  • routes: Array of route numbers serving this stop
  • nearbyStopIDs: Array of stop IDs at the same coordinates
  • data_timestamp: Last update time
